🎄 PVE Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony

A cherished annual community tradition celebrating the beginning of the holiday season.


Event Overview

Location: Historic Malaga Cove Plaza
Address: 43 Malaga Cove Plaza, Palos Verdes Estates, CA

Join us for one of the most cherished and heartwarming traditions in Palos Verdes Estates! Held each year at the beautiful historic Malaga Cove Plaza, this festive community gathering lights up the holiday season with joy, togetherness, and plenty of holiday cheer. The event typically takes place in early December from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM, offering free hot cocoa, delightful holiday music, letters to Santa, and the magical lighting of the city’s Christmas tree.

Festive Schedule of Events

The evening generally follows a family-friendly schedule of events leading up to the main lighting:

Time Typical Activity
4:30 PM – 6:00 PM Roaming holiday carolers fill the air with joyful songs as the sun sets over the plaza.
6:00 PM – 6:20 PM Performance by the The talented Rolling Hills United Methodist Church Handbell Choir delivers beautiful, melodic holiday performances..
6:20 PM – 6:30 PM The Mayor offers a warm welcome to the community.
6:30 PM Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony The highlight of the evening — the grand tree lighting! Watch as the plaza sparkles to life in a brilliant display of holiday magic.

Family Fun & Preparations

Throughout the evening, local organizations and volunteers run several stationary activities for all ages:

🍪 Cookie Decorating

A sweet tradition hosted by our enthusiastic local Girl Scout troops.

🎅 Letters to Santa

A favorite highlight lovingly managed and run by the Palos Verdes Estates Neighborhood Watch. This beloved activity brings pure delight to children of all ages as they excitedly share their holiday wishes — a heartwarming tradition that creates lasting memories for families year after year.

🏛️ Preparation

For weeks leading up to the event, the City of Palos Verdes Estates, the PVE Foundation, and the dedicated Malaga Cove and Lunada Bay homeowners associations work tirelessly to transform the plaza and ensure every light on the tree shines brightly.
