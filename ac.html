How You Can Get Involved

Become an Area Coordinator:

A Neighborhood Watch Area Coordinator serves as the primary liaison between residents, Block Captains, law enforcement, and the Neighborhood Watch organization. The Area Coordinator helps recruit and support volunteers, shares safety information, promotes community engagement, and works to strengthen neighborhood communication and crime prevention efforts within their assigned area.
