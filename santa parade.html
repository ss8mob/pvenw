🎅 Santa's Parade

A cherished annual community tradition celebrating the holiday season.


Event Overview

Location: Historic Malaga Cove Plaza
Address: 43 Malaga Cove Plaza, Palos Verdes Estates, CA

At the heart of this cherished tradition is the PVE Neighborhood Watch, whose dedicated volunteers pour their hearts into making the evening unforgettable for families young and old. Watch for them in Malaga Cove Plaza — they’ll be the ones keeping you warm with steaming cups of hot apple cider while you wait for Santa to arrive! And for the little ones with something important to say to the Big Guy? The Neighborhood Watch proudly carries on the treasured tradition of Letters to Santa, giving every child the chance to put their wishes into writing. It’s just one of the many ways our Neighborhood Watch shows up — not just to keep our community safe, but to make it shine.

🎅 Santa's Parade Schedule

Here’s how the magic typically unfolds on parade night:

  • 4:30 PM ---> The Journey Begins

    Santa sets off from 520 Paseo Lunado (just steps from Lunada Bay Elementary School) at approximately 4:30 PM, making his merry way toward Malaga Cove Plaza.

  • 5:45 PM – 7:00 PM ---> Meet & Greet with Santa

    From roughly 5:45 PM to 7:00 PM, Santa holds court at Malaga Cove Plaza — the perfect opportunity for hugs, photos, and whispered wish lists! Neighborhood Watch volunteers and the PVE Police Department are on hand to host a wonderful family celebration filled with warmth, laughter, and holiday cheer.

  • 7:15 PM – 8:00 PM ---> The Grand Finale

    At 7:15 PM, the parade rolls on eastbound along PV Drive North through the charming Valmonte neighborhood, wrapping up at the PVE Civic Center around 8:00 PM — a perfect ending to a perfect evening.
